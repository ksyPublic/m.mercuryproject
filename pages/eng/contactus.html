Setting up media KPI goals of campaigns. Planning and buying the media to align with KPI goals Managing and analyzing the integrated data to prove the result.

The candidates should be familiar with data-driven communications. The candidates should be keen to understand the comprehensive ad-tech solutions. The candidates should actively absorb the new market trend. The candidates should be professional at the documentation. The candidates who have carrier experiences of either a media planner or an account executive at advertising agencies are preferred.

Analyzing the market trends and brand positionings. Planning the target segmentation and campaign strategies. Planning channel contents strategies and operating.

The candidates should have comprehensive understandings about brands and markets. The candidates should make an effort not for the persuasions but the solutions.
